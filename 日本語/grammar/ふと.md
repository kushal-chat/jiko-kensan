
==試行錯誤の末、彼は**ふと**正しい答えを思いついた。==
After much trial and error, he **suddenly** came up with the correct answer.

==子供の頃の出来事を**不図**思い出した。==
I **suddenly** remembered events from my childhood.

==歳をとるに連れて、夜中に**不図**目が覚めて睡眠の質が下がることが多くなった。==
As I get older, I often wake up **unexpectedly** in the middle of the night, and the quality of my sleep has decreased.
