〜にかかわらず and 〜にもかかわらず look similar but have different meanings and uses.
〜にかかわらず means “regardless of” and is used when the outcome or action is not affected by a certain condition.
〜にもかかわらず means “despite” or “although” and is used to express contrast between two ideas.
