を元に 'based on' differs to に基づいて 'based on', in that に基づいて indicates a foundation from which the judgement or conclusion of (B) is made, while を元に focuses more on being a source of inspiration for (B), or enabling (B).

==彼は法律に基づいて処分されるだろう==。
He will be receiving punishment **according to** the law.

==彼は法律をもとに処分されるだろう==。
He will be punished **based on** the law.

## 志

==もともとは別の仕事を行なっていたのですが「困っている人を助けたい」の**志を元に**、社会人ながらも大学へ入学し、資格を取得して社会福祉士となりました。==
Although I was originally working in a different field, driven by the **desire to help people in need**, I enrolled in university while still working full-time, obtained the necessary qualifications, and became a social worker.
