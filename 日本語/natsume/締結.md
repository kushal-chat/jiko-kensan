## 契約

==新卒住宅補助制度とは、自己名義で賃借契約を**締結**し、家賃を全額自己負担する方へ入社後3年間補助を支給する制度です。==
The housing allowance system for new graduates is a program that provides financial assistance for three years after joining the company to those who **sign** a lease agreement in their own name and pay the full rent themselves.
